## Ideas

- [ ] **Multi-phone body capture for dance ↔ music**
  - Mount multiple phones on the body (e.g. 2 on arms, 2 on legs) to capture human movement.
  - Use simple dance moves to drive a music composition.
  - Two directions of use:
    - **Dance → Music:** perform/learn music by dancing (movement composes/triggers sound).
    - **Music → Dance:** practice dance to existing music (movement is guided by the score).
